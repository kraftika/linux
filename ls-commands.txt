To remember:

1. echo *
2. echo */
3. ls -a --- list hidden files
4. ls -l --- list in long format
5. ls --author -l --- list the author for each file
6. ls --block-size=M -l --- list in desired scale format
7. ls -cl --- list the files sorted and show tha last associated modification
8. ls -ltc --- list the files sorted and show tha last associated modification and the time of the modification
9. ls --color=always
10. ls -d --- list only directories
